## Execution Flow

1. **Understand the activity**
   - What's being done?
   - Who's affected?
   - What's the context?

2. **Identify risk categories**
   - Regulatory
   - Contractual
   - Litigation
   - Employment
   - Privacy
   - IP

3. **Assess each risk**
   - Probability
   - Impact
   - Combined level

4. **Recommend mitigations**
   - Prioritized actions
   - Owners
   - Timelines

5. **Flag for escalation**
