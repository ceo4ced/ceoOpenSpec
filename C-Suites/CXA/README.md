## 6. Crisis Communication Plan

### 6.1 Crisis Categories

| Category | Examples | Severity | Lead |
|----------|----------|----------|------|
| Service Outage | System down, major feature broken | Critical | CTO |
| Security Incident | Data breach, vulnerability | Critical | CIO |
| PR Crisis | Negative press, social media storm | High | CMO |
| Legal Issue | Lawsuit, regulatory action | High | CLO |
| Customer Incident | Major customer complaint | Medium | CXA |

### 6.2 Communication Timeline

| Timeframe | Action | Owner |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| 0-15 min | Acknowledge internally | Incident team |
| 15-30 min | Initial external communication | CXA |
| 30-60 min | Status page update | CTO + CXA |
| Hourly | Progress updates | CXA |
| Resolution | Resolution notification | CXA |
| Post-incident | Post-mortem communication | CEO |

### 6.3 Crisis Templates

| Template | Use Case | Approval Required |
|----------|----------|-------------------|
| Service Disruption | Outages, degraded performance | CTO |
| Security Notification | Breaches, vulnerabilities | CEO + CLO |
| Apology | Service failures, mistakes | CEO |
| Legal Hold | Regulatory, legal matters | CLO |

### 6.4 Stakeholder Communication

| Stakeholder | Channel | Timing | Owner |
|-------------|---------|--------|-------|
| All Customers | Email + Status Page | Immediate | CXA |
| Enterprise Customers | Email + Phone | Immediate | CXA |
| Board | Email | Within 1 hour | CEO |
| Press | Prepared statement | As needed | CMO |
| Employees | Slack/Email | Immediate | CEO |

### 6.5 Post-Incident Review

| Element | Content |
|---------|---------|
| Summary | What happened |
| Timeline | Sequence of events |
| Impact | Who was affected, how |
| Root Cause | Why it happened |
| Resolution | How it was fixed |
| Prevention | What we're doing to prevent recurrence |
