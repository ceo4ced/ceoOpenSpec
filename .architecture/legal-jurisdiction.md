# Legal Jurisdiction Framework

## Overview

This document establishes the legal compliance framework that applies to ALL agents in the business.

> **Ignorance of the law is not an excuse. Agents must comply with ALL applicable laws.**

---

## Hierarchy of Law

### 1. International Treaties & Agreements
- Trade agreements affecting business
- Tax treaties
- Data transfer frameworks (EU-US Data Privacy Framework)

### 2. Federal/National Law
- United States federal law (primary if US-incorporated)
- Other national laws where operating

### 3. State/Provincial Law
- All US states where we:
  - Have employees
  - Have customers
  - Process data
  - Do business
  
### 4. Local/Municipal Law
- City and county ordinances
- Zoning (if physical presence)

---

## Universal Legal Requirements

These apply to ALL businesses, regardless of industry:

### Business Formation & Governance
- Corporate governance law
- Registered agent requirements
- Annual filings
- Business licenses

### Employment & Labor
- Fair Labor Standards Act (federal)
- State labor codes
- Worker classification (employee vs contractor)
- Wage and hour laws
- Anti-discrimination laws

### Tax
- Federal income tax
- State income tax (where applicable)
- Payroll taxes
- Sales/use tax (state by state)
- International tax (if applicable)

### Consumer Protection
- FTC Act (no deceptive practices)
- State consumer protection laws
- Warranty requirements
- Refund policies

### Privacy & Data
- State privacy laws (CCPA, etc.)
- Breach notification requirements
- Data minimization principles

### Contracts & Commerce
- UCC (Uniform Commercial Code)
- Electronic signature laws (ESIGN, UETA)
- Contract formation requirements

## Industry-Specific Triggers

### If Handling Health Data
```yaml
healthcare_triggers:
  - HIPAA
  - State health privacy laws
  - FDA regulations (if SaMD)
```

### If Handling Financial Data
```yaml
financial_triggers:
  - GLBA
  - State financial regulations
  - PCI-DSS (if credit cards)
  - SEC/FINRA (if securities)
```

### If Serving Children
```yaml
children_triggers:
  - COPPA
  - State child protection laws
  - CARU guidelines
```

### If Using AI
```yaml
ai_triggers:
  - NYC Local Law 144 (if hiring AI)
  - Colorado AI Governance Act
  - EU AI Act (if EU users)
  - State AI disclosure laws
```

## Geographic Expansion Checklist

When entering a new market (state or country):

```markdown
### Pre-Entry Analysis

- [ ] Identify all applicable laws
- [ ] Determine registration requirements
- [ ] Assess tax nexus/obligations
- [ ] Review employment law differences
- [ ] Check data protection requirements
- [ ] Verify marketing compliance
- [ ] Confirm product/service legality
- [ ] Establish local agent if required

### CLO Sign-Off Required

Before expansion, CLO must approve:
- Jurisdiction analysis complete
- Compliance plan documented
- Resources allocated
- Ongoing monitoring plan
```

---

## Agent Responsibilities

### All Agents MUST:
- Check with CLO before new activities
- Comply with CLO compliance directives
- Report potential legal issues immediately
- Not make legal representations

### CLO MUST:
- Monitor legal developments in all jurisdictions
- Provide compliance guidance to departments
- Escalate legal risks to CEO/Human
- Maintain jurisdiction tracking
- Review all external-facing content for legal compliance

---
