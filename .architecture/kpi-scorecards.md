# McKinsey-Aligned KPI Scorecards

## Overview

These scorecards are based on McKinsey & Company's recommended performance metrics for e-commerce and digital businesses.

Each position is measured against metrics they can directly influence.

---

## CEO Scorecard

### Strategic Leadership Metrics

| KPI | Description | Target | Frequency |
|-----|-------------|--------|-----------|
| **Revenue Growth Rate** | YoY revenue growth | ≥20% | Monthly |
| **Gross Margin** | (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue | ≥60% | Monthly |
| **EBITDA Margin** | Operating profitability | ≥15% | Quarterly |
| **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)** | Total value per customer | Growing | Monthly |
| **CAC Payback Period** | Months to recover acquisition cost | ≤12 months | Monthly |
| **Agent Efficiency Score** | Decisions per token cost | Improving | Weekly |
| **Mission Alignment Index** | % of activities aligned to mission | ≥90% | Weekly |
| **Stakeholder NPS** | Human satisfaction with agents | ≥50 | Monthly |
