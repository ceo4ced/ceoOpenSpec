# cmo.campaign

## Preamble

This command creates marketing campaign plans with budget allocation, targeting, and success metrics.

**Must read before execution:**
1. `.mission/agent-governance.md`
2. `CMO/.ethics/ethics.md`
3. `CEO/.ceo/memory/business-plan.md` (if exists)
4. `CMO/.cmo/memory/strategy.md` (if exists)

---

## Execution Flow

1. **Define objective**
   - What are we trying to achieve?
   - How will we measure success?

2. **Identify audience**
   - Who are we targeting?
   - Where do they spend time?

3. **Select channels**
   - Which channels fit audience?
   - What's the budget allocation?

4. **Plan creative**
   - What message?
   - What assets needed?

5. **Set budget**
   - Total and breakdown
   - Kill criteria

6. **Validate compliance**
   - FTC requirements
   - Platform policies
   - Privacy laws
