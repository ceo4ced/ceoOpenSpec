## Outline

The CEO Plan command transforms the gathered vision into a structured business plan that becomes the root README.md of the business repository.

### Execution Flow

1. **Validate Prerequisites**
   
   Check for required files:
   - `CEO/.ceo/memory/vision.md` MUST exist
   - If missing: "Run /ceo.vision first to gather the business vision."

2. **Load Context**
   
   - Parse vision.md for key elements
   - Load any existing business plan (README.md root)
   - Identify what needs to be created vs. updated

4. **Research Integration**
   
   Where possible, enhance the plan with:
   - Market size estimates (cite sources)
   - Competitor analysis (cite sources)
   - Industry benchmarks (cite sources)
   
   All research must include citations.

5. **Populate .mission Files**
   
   If `.mission/` files are still templates, generate drafts:
   - `mission-statement.md` - Extract from vision
   - `values.md` - Infer from founder's priorities
   - `objective.md` - Primary measurable goal
   - `elevator-pitch.md` - From solution and UVP
   
   Mark as DRAFT for human review.

6. **Validation**
   
   Before finalizing:
   - [ ] All sections have content (no placeholders)
   - [ ] Research has citations
   - [ ] High-risk domains are flagged
   - [ ] C-suite status table is accurate
   - [ ] Timeline is realistic
   - [ ] Next steps are actionable

7. **Output**

   - Update `CEO/.ceo/memory/plan-version.md` with version info
   - Log the generation to `CEO/logs/`
   - Suggest next step: `/ceo.propagate` to distribute to C-suite

## Business Plan Quality Criteria

The business plan must:

1. **Be Evidence-Based**: Include market data and research with citations
2. **Be Realistic**: Timelines and targets should be achievable
3. **Be Complete**: All sections filled (no TBD or placeholder text)
4. **Identify Risks**: Flag regulatory and market risks explicitly
5. **Be Actionable**: Clear next steps for each C-suite position
6. **Gate the CTO**: CTO section must show "Gated" status

---

## Escalation Rules

Escalate to founder if:
- Market research reveals significant competition concerns
- Regulatory requirements are more complex than initially understood
- Timeline seems unrealistic given scope
- Budget may be insufficient for stated goals
